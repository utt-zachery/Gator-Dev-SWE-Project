Mission


At PassItOn, we believe that running a food bank is hard enough without worrying about tracking inventory, managing orders, and item nutrition. At PassItOn, we beleive in empowering users tools to make healthy lifestyle decisons. Our software is designed for the manager and the consumer alike, such that the application does the heavy lifitng of running a food bank.

We belive that extending access to food bank services improved the life and well being for Americans across the country. We beleive that simplifying the food donation process encourages new donors to give back to there communities. And we beleive that real change comes when communties - rather than for profit coprorations - are empowered.
